About

I'm Mohamed Taha, a creative graphic designer passionate about transforming ideas into stunning visuals! With a strong artistic sense, I craft designs that express brand identities professionally and modernly. I excel in logo design, visual identity, advertising, and social media using Canva, Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. My goal is to bring clients' visions to life with unique and innovative designs. Let's create something amazing

My Skills

  • Image Editing and Processing – Optimizing and modifying images with high quality and professionalism.
  • Visual Identity Design – Creating logos, business profiles, and consistent colors and fonts.
  • Social Media Ads – Designing professional publications suitable for various platforms.
  • Print Design – Business cards, brochures, posters, packaging, and magazines.
  • Proficiency in design software – Experience in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and After Effects
